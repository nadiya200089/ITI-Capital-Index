our people

Leonardo Brummas Carvalho

CEO Wealth Management

Leonardo has 23 years of experience in the financial services industry. He has held executive positions with Credit Suisse for 22 years. At his previous role he was Head of Emerging Europe London from 2013 to 2019 with a focus on Russia and the CIS. In 2019 Leonardo was invited to ITI Capital as CEO Wealth Management. His is responsible for restructuring the group, further digitization and accelerating its growth.

Rahul Agarwal

Managing Director

Rahul has over 20 years of experience within the financial sector working at firms such as TD Ameritrade in America and then ETRADE, Saxo Capital Markets and IG in the UK. Most recently he was at Societe Generale Securities Services in the UK where he was involved in the Wealth Management offering.

At ITI Capital, Rahul will be responsible for the profitability of the private client business including product, sales & marketing and dealing.

Andrey Chuprin

Head of Financing, Treasury & Structured Products

Andrey has over 20 years of experience in financial sector joining us from Nomura where he was a Managing Director, Head of Russia & CIS Global Markets Sales. Prior to that he was a Managing Director, heading FICC Sales at Sberbank CIB as well as Director at Credit Suisse and Merrill Lynch.

At ITI Capital, Andrey oversees an important area of capital and liquidity management across all locations. He also focuses on growing our capital markets and financing capabilities for various client segments.

Steve Farrel

Director Institutional Equity Sales and Trading

Steve has over 30 years experience in various financial services client facing roles. He started his career in 1986 at Salomon Brothers International as a trading floor runner, shortly after moving to S.G Warburg on the European Market Making desk. Subsequent roles included serving 7 years at Instinet London as an Institutional Equity Sales Trader and Liquidnet Limited as Co-Head of EMEA Sales and Head of the Equity Trading Desk.

Philipp Panov

Head of ITI Tech

Philipp has been working in the financial market since 1993. Prior to joining ITI Capital as a Managing Director, he was responsible for team-building and sales in Uralsib Capital and Renaissance Capital, interacting with institutional, corporate, private (HNWI) clients. Since joining ITI Capital in 2018, Philipp has been developing the group’s business in Russia.

Anatoly Gorlov

Head of ITI Capital Russia

Rafael Zanatta

Director – HFT / DMA Sales

Rafael Zanatta joined ITI Capital to develop the Direct Market Access business into Russian and worldwide markets focusing on low latency and algorithmic trading solutions. Previously he worked at Eurex Exchange where for over 5 years he covered Equity & Equity Index Derivatives Sales in the UK, Ireland and Iberian markets. Rafael graduated from London Metropolitan University with a first class Bachelor’s Degree in European Banking & Finance.

Svetlana Musseau

Director Structured Products

Svetlana began her career in 2005 working for Deutsche Bank on an exotic derivatives desk and has since worked in client solutions teams across such banks as BNP Paribas and Goldman Sachs. She has focused on and developed businesses across structuring, structured financing, special-situation investments and corporate hedging solutions. Svetlana holds a Masters in Finance honours degree from Cass Business School in London.
